"LIMA, May 01. /ITAR-TASS/. Peru is interested in establishing closer contacts with the Customs Union that brings together Belarus, Russia and Kazakhstan, RF Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov said on Thursday after talks with Peruvian Foreign Minister Eda Rivas.

“Our partners from Peru are interested in making closer contacts with the Customs Union, even free trade agreements,” Lavrov said. “I think that as the relations develop we will engage in discussing these matters.”

The Russian foreign minister invited the Peruvian colleague to visit Moscow at any time convenient for her. “We hope to continue the contacts,” he added."
