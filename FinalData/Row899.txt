"BRUSSELS, May 16. /ITAR-TASS/. A three-party ministerial meeting on Ukraine’s gas debt may take place on May 26, a spokesperson for the European Commission said. Russia’s Energy Minister Alexander Novak, Ukraine’s parliament-appointed Energy Minister Yuriy Prodan and European Commissioner for Energy Guenther Oettinger are expected to participate in the meeting.

The press service of the European Commission said other dates would be discussed as well, but May 26 is being negotiated right now."
