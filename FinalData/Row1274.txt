"MOSCOW, May 03. /ITAR-TASS/. President Vladimir Putin on Saturday, May 3, sent a message of condolences to President of the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan Hamid Karzai in connection with the numerous casualties and damage caused by a landslide in the Badakhshan province.

The President of Russia addressed words of compassion and support to the victims’ families and wished speedy recovery to all those injured."
