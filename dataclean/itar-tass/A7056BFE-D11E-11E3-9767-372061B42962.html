Russia continues supplying Russian nuclear fuel to Ukrainian power plants Energoatom Energoatom operates the four existing nuclear power plants in Ukraine Ukraine Sweden extend contract on nuclear fuel supplies for Ukrainian NPP KIEV April 23. /ITAR-TASS/. Russia continues supplying nuclear fuel to Ukraines nuclear power plants. According to Yuri Nedashkovsky president of the Energoatom Company Ukraines three major nuclear power plants continue delivering spent nuclear fuel to Russia for temporary storage in a normal regime. We have no problems at present including in taking (spent fuel) out of the country Nedashkovsky told ITAR-TASS on Wednesday. Energoatom operates the four existing nuclear power plants in Ukraine and 15 power units with aggregate electrical power of 13835 GW.
