MOSCOW April 26 (Itar-Tass) - There have been no violations of Ukraines air space by Russian warplanes the Russian Defence Ministry has stated. Russian systems of objective monitoring of the air situation have not detected any violations of air borders of states neighbouring Russia including Ukraine the ministry said in a statement on Saturday in response to the statements of Pentagon officials that a Russian fighter allegedly had violated Ukraines air space.
