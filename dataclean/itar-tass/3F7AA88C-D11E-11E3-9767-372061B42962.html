EU signs 1 billion euro ($1.4 billion) memorandum in aid for Ukraine BRUSSELS April 28. /ITAR-TASS/. Representatives of the EU and Ukraine will sign a memorandum of allocating 1 billion euro ($1.4 billion) Ukraine financial assistance the European Commission representative Pia Hansen Arenkilde said. "We (April 28) will sign a memorandum of understanding regarding the allocation of 1 billion euro Ukraine within the framework of the program of financial assistance" she said. The representative of the European Commission did not specify where the ceremony take place and who of the representatives of the parties take part in it.
