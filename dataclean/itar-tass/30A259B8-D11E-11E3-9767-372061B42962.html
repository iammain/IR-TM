Gunmen cache with more than tonne of food found in Ingushetia MOSCOW April 27 /ITAR-TASS/. Gunmens cache with more than a tonne of food products has been found in Russias North Caucasian Republic of Ingushetia the information centre of the RF National Anti-Terrorism Committee (NAC) told Itar-Tass. The Russian Federal Security Service (FSB) department for Ingushetia jointly with the Interior Troops found in the Ali-Yurt settlement of the Nazran district a cache - dugout that belonged to members of illegal armed groups a NAC official said. The cache contents were destroyed at the site a search operation has been launched near the cache the NAC said.
