New sanctions against Russia not to help solve Ukrainian crisis The sanctions do not facilitate national dialogue disarmament and withdrawal of military units Russias Permanent Representative to OSCE Andrei Kelin says VIENNA April 28. /ITAR-TASS/. New sanctions against Russia will not help to solve the problems facing Ukraine Russias Permanent Representative to the Organisation for Security and Cooperation in Europe (OSCE) Andrei Kelin said on Monday April 28. The sanctions are the doing of Washington and Brussels but they do not solve the problems of Ukraine and do not facilitate national dialogue disarmament and withdrawal of military units he said.
