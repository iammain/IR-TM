No instructions for retaliatory restrictions on Ukrainians to enter Russia minister BIROBIDZHAN April 24. /ITAR-TASS/. No retaliatory restrictions were imposed at transport facilities for Ukrainian citizens to enter Russia Russian Transport Minister Maxim Sokolov told reporters on Thursday. The issue of response to sanctions is within thejurisdiction of the countrys Foreign Ministry the minister added. No instructions were given to us over operation of transport he stated.
