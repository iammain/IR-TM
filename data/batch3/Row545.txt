"MOSCOW, April 27. /ITAR-TASS/. Moscow’s police are checking information about a bomb planted at the Moscow-based Mosfilm film studio, a spokesman for the Moscow police department told Itar-Tass on Sunday.

“Today, at about 15:40, the Moscow police received an anonymous telephone call warning about an explosive device planted at Mosfilm,” the spokesman said.

The police have evacuated all people from the site. Specialists with dogs are inspecting the premises."
