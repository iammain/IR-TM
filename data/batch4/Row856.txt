"MOSCOW, May 18 /ITAR-TASS/. Ukrainian law enforcers have detained a group of TV journalists from Russian media holding LifeNews near the south-eastern Ukrainian city of Kramatorsk, the TV holding said in a statement transmitted to Itar-Tass on Sunday.

“Reporters were presumably stopped at a checkpoint near the city of Kramatorsk. There is no cellular phone communications with them,” the statement runs."
