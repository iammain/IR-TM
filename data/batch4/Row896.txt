"MOSCOW, April 26 (Itar-Tass) - Russia within available possibilities was taking steps to settle the situation over the detention of military inspectors near Slavyansk, the Russian Foreign Ministry said on Saturday.

They were invited by the Ukrainian authorities, in accordance with the 2011 Vienna document on confidence and security strengthening measures. Under the document, the inviting side is responsible for ensuring security for inspectors, the ministry notes."
