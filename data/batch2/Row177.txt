"IVANOVO, May 26. /ITAR-TASS/. Russia respects the choice of the Ukrainian people, Sergei Ivanov, Head of Kremlin Administration, told journalists on Monday.

The Kremlin is ready for Ukraine’s lawsuit over Crimea’s accession to Russia, Ivanov added.

“We will be waiting for a lawsuit in the case if he (Petro Poroshenko, who is leading in Ukraine’s Sunday’s presidential elections) is going (to file it),” Ivanov told journalists."
