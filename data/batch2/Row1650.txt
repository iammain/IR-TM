"“Russian Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov and Austrian Federal Minister of European and International Affairs Sebastian Kurz, who chairs the Committee of Ministers of the Council of Europe, met on the sidelines of a 124th session of the Council of Ministers of the Council of Europe in Vienna,” the Russian diplomatic agency said.

“The ministers focused on the current situation in Ukraine, discussed ways out from current crisis in the country, Council of Europe’s role in assisting the process of normalization in the country,” the country’s Foreign Ministry added."
