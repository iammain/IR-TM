"BERLIN, May 05. /ITAR-TASS/. Germany does not have any plans in the short term to send its military observers to Ukraine as part of a new mission under the auspices of the Organization for Security and Cooperation in Europe (OSCE), a spokesman for the German Defense Ministry said on Monday.

He said Bundeswehr servicemen would not be sent to Ukraine as observers at least until the May 25 presidential election in the country."
