"MOSCOW, May 30. /ITAR-TASS/. Russia’s Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov has expressed hope that the OSCE “roadmap” on Ukraine will be discussed at talks with Slovenian counterpart Karl Erjavec.

“I hope for trust-based discussions on the situation in Ukraine,” Lavrov said on Friday.

“It is necessary to start implementing the OSCE ‘roadmap’ as soon as possible. It will help stop tensions in Ukraine and launch a nationwide dialogue on the constitutional reform,” he said."
