"MOSCOW, May 19. /ITAR-TASS/. Russian Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov urged the Organisation for Security and Co-operation in Europe (OSCE) to use urgently all possibilities of this organisation to release Russian journalists from LifeNews TV channel in Ukraine, the Russian Foreign Ministry said on Monday.

“Similar demarches were also made before the leadership of OSCE Secretariat in Vienna and the OSCE mission headquarters in Kiev, Ukraine,” the Russian ministry reported.

“The Russian Ministry of Foreign Affairs will be seeking the soonest settling of the situation around the Russian journalists,” the diplomatic service said."
